Sequins, Pearls & Jets

Unique Decorations That Express Your Personal Style

We offer a wide range of high-quality sequins, pearls and rhinestones so you can create designs that will add the right accent. Our sequins of various diameters (3mm and 5mm) and in a wide range of colors add unique shine to every project.

Elegance in Details: Pearls Tailored to Your Preferences

For lovers of classics, we offer pearls with a diameter of 4 mm to 8 mm, in various shades, such as pearl, black or gold. We are flexible and ready to adapt to your individual color preferences, guaranteeing elegant details in every project.

Unlimited Possibilities of Rhinestones for Intense Shine

Our ultrasonically glued rhinestones in various sizes and colors offer unlimited creative possibilities. Add intense shine and sparkle to your designs. If you dream of a unique pattern or composition of stones, we are ready to meet your expectations.

Individualize Your Projects with Our Services

Not only do we offer a wide range of sequins, pearls and rhinestones, but we also allow you to sew on sequins so that you can individualize your designs. Our assortment also includes reversible sequins, which add additional charm to your compositions.

Immerse yourself in the world of possibilities and create projects that will stand out for their originality. Our services are dedicated to everyone who wants to express their creativity through unique decorations. Give your designs a unique character with our sequins, pearls and rhinestones!
