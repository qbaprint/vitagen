Sublimation Printing

Transfer printing is characterized by high durability - it is indelible.

Prints can have an unlimited number of very high colors resolution. Possibility of polishing and gold-plating the whole thing or fragment of the print.

We offer a transfer printing service on fabrics and knitted fabrics. We develop patterns in own graphic studio. We print:
  • on square footage
  • cutouts of any size
We also apply transfer printing in the form of applications to cutouts cotton, viscose, silk and other raw materials.
We combine the transfer printing technique with embroidery and flocking pastings.
We decorate the print with sequins, pearls, rhinestones, stones and more decorations ordered by the customer.
